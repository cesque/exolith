The following are my 31 entries for **MÖRKTOBER 2022**: a set of microfiction entries and ideas inspired by, and for use in, tabletop roleplaying games. They are more fiction than RPG supplement, and you won't find any stat blocks included nor dice rolls mentioned. Hopefully, though, you will find some intriguing ideas for your upcoming campaign or one-shot.

These were written *mostly* in October of 2022, with a few stragglers spaced out over the rest of the following 12 months. Each entry follows a specific theme from the following **MÖRKTOBER 2022** list, in order:
